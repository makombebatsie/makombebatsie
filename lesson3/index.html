Community Forecast

Empowerment through planning

Site Name

The site name of this site will be Community Forecast. As the term forecast suggests, the site will carry information of all that happens within the community of Preston.

Site purpose

The purpose of the website is to primarily inform the audience or users of possible weather condition predictions for up to five days ahead. This enables people to prepare ahead and put everything in order for better convenience.
However, the site is not limited to weather forecast alone but also informs of upcoming events, ideal places to visit, historical sites etc.

Target Audience And Scenarios

The site accomodates everyone who might be wanting to visit the community from families, business associates scheduled for a meeting, students coming on a field trip, those seeking to transact together etc.

For example looking at students who is to visit:
They come seeking more knowledge be it on weather or something similar.
May not have any current income.
Ages may rate from 12-23.
Possibly will just be visiting for a day.

Taking Regional Officer as another example:
Aged probably between 35-50. May have an annual salary of $50 000
Purpose of travel might be for a business meeting that involves how they can improve the community and the region as a whole

Scenarios may include:
What do i need to carry?
How is the weather going to be like?
Are there any accomodation places close to where Im going?
